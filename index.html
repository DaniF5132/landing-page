More info about Pastafarianism

The Flying Spaghetti Monster (FSM) is the deity of the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster, or Pastafarianism, a social movement that promotes a light-hearted view of religion.

The Jolly Fish (or Pirate Fish) is a secondary logo associated with the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
The Jolly Fish symbolises the Pastafarian affinity with pirates.
